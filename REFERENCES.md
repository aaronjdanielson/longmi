# References

The canonical, annotated citation list lives in
[docs/references.md](docs/references.md) (also rendered in the
documentation site), covering: missing-data foundations, multiple
imputation and posterior-predictive draws, combining rules and degrees of
freedom, congeniality and estimating-equation theory, GEE and marginal
models, inverse probability weighting, MNAR sensitivity analysis,
numerical methods, simulation-evaluation methodology, the applied tutorial
and data sources, and the software longmi validates against.

longmi implements established missing-data theory; it does not introduce a
new proof of multiple imputation.
